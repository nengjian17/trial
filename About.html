AP Oil – Our Journey

1975 – Foundation

Incorporated as Huan Chew Oil Trading Pte Ltd, distributing lubricants in Singapore.

1981 – Manufacturing Begins

Launched the first Singaporean-owned lubricant manufacturing plant.

1988 – Second Plant & Jetty

Established Alpha Pacific Petroleum with a 3,000 MT jetty and 6,000 MT storage terminal.

This expansion greatly enhanced our logistics capacity and marine services.

2000 – Specialty Chemicals

Acquired A.I.M Chemical Industries Pte Ltd to enter specialty chemical production.

Provided toll-blending and contract manufacturing services for multinational companies.

2001 – Public Listing

AP Oil was listed on the Singapore Stock Exchange, initiating a new growth era.

2003 – Mainboard Upgrade

Upgraded to the SGX Mainboard, enhancing corporate visibility and investor confidence.

Today – Global Operations

We operate 6 manufacturing plants and export to over 20 countries worldwide.

  • 3 lubricant plants (2 with private jetties)
  • 2 specialty chemical facilities
