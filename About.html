AP Oil – Our Journey

1975

Foundation

AP Oil International Limited was incorporated as Huan Chew Oil Trading Pte Ltd, starting as a lubricant distributor in Singapore.

1981

Manufacturing Begins

We expanded into manufacturing, establishing the first Singaporean-owned lubricant plant in the Republic.

1988

Second Plant & Private Jetty

A major milestone with the founding of Alpha Pacific Petroleum (S) Pte Ltd, featuring a private jetty capable of berthing vessels up to 3,000 metric tonnes and a storage terminal with 6,000 metric tonnes capacity.

2000

Specialty Chemicals Expansion

Acquired A.I.M Chemical Industries Pte Ltd, moving into specialty chemicals with a blending plant offering toll-blending and contract manufacturing mainly for multinational companies.

2001

Public Listing

AP Oil was listed on the Singapore Stock Exchange, marking a new growth chapter.

2003

Mainboard Upgrade

Upgraded to the SGX Mainboard, enhancing our corporate visibility and investor confidence.

Today

Global Operations

Operating six manufacturing plants—three for lubricants (two with private jetties) and two for specialty chemicals—our products reach over 20 countries across Asia and beyond, including Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, China, Japan, and Middle East countries.
