Who We Are

AP Oil International Limited, formerly known as Huan Chew Oil Trading Pte Ltd, was incorporated in 1975 as a lubricant distributor. We ventured into manufacturing in 1981 and set up the first Singaporean-owned lubricant plant in the Republic.
