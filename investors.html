About AP Oil International

Incorporated in Singapore on 24 December 1975 under the name Huan Chew Oil Trading Pte Ltd, the company became a public entity in 2001 and was renamed AP Oil International Ltd.

We specialize in manufacturing lubricating oils and fluids for industrial, automotive, and marine use, while also trading base oils and additives.

Through AIM Chemical Industries (Pte) Ltd, our subsidiary, we offer toll blending and contract manufacturing for specialty chemicals, as well as chemical trading.

Listed on the Singapore Stock Exchange in 2001 and promoted to the Mainboard in 2003, AP Oil is recognized in the lubricants and specialty chemicals industry.

We operate three plants in Singapore, including a lubricant plant with its own oil terminal and private jetty capable of handling vessels up to 15,000 tons.

Performance by Operating Segments

Manufacturing

In FY2024, manufacturing represented 70% or $38.4 million (FY2023: 67% or $30.9 million) of the Group’s revenue. This includes the blending of lubricating oils and specialty chemicals.

The Group operates three blending plants — two wholly-owned in Singapore and one joint venture in Vietnam (AP Saigon Petro JSC), producing lubricants under its brands “AP Oil” and “SIN-O”.

Specialty chemicals are manufactured by subsidiaries A.I.M. Chemical Industries Pte Ltd and GB Chemicals Pte Ltd.

Trading

The trading segment includes purchasing and selling raw materials (mainly base oils, chemicals, and finished goods). It contributed 30% or $16.3 million of Group revenue in FY2024 (FY2023: 33% or $15.3 million).
